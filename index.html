About Me

Hi! I'm Aqsa Maryim, a Korean drama watcher and a dedicated learner of the Korean language. I enjoy watching K-dramas not just for entertainment, but also as a way to improve my language skills — and let's be honest, it’s a bonus that most people around me can’t understand what’s being said!

Learning Korean has been a unique and exciting journey, and it truly feels like a superpower sometimes. Apart from that, I can drive a car confidently, and I’ve always wanted to learn how to ride a motorcycle. It's not that it's too hard, but due to a few personal reasons, I haven’t had the chance to learn yet.

I also deeply appreciate nature — its peaceful beauty, the soothing sound of birds, and the calmness of rainy days or stormy skies. I find joy in experiencing these quiet moments alone or sharing them with someone special — someone who truly cares and understands me.
